Learn & play with Kiu

Learning with Kiu is entertaining. Earn points for correct answers and level up

There's a need for 160 Malaysian Sign Language (MSL) interpreter to reach the ratio of 1:300 Deaf people in the country. Unfortunately, working as an interpreter is financially challenging and learning this language is inaccessible to most.

We created Kiu to give everyone access to a private tutor experience. It is a free, fun signing education at the tip of your finger. Any time, any where.

We believe in developing technology to improve communication equality by removing existing language barriers.

Our Experts

S.I.D
  • More often known by its acronym S.I.D., is a registered non-profit & non-governmental voluntary organization founded in 1990. S.I.D. takes on the mission to serve as interpreters for Kiu.
RC Deaf Missions
  • RC Deaf Missions was founded in 2006, providing career opportunities to many Deaf persons. They add value to Kiu by creating community awareness projects, Deaf mentoring, and role modelling.
Hati.my
  • Hati.my is run by volunteers made up of journalists, entrepreneurs, and programmers. They recommend beneficiaries to run CSR programmes and promote volunteers drive.
